JQuery Educational is a website dedicated to showcasing some of the more complex tools at our disposal when using the Jquery Library. From across corners of the internet we have plucked those curious little functions that we think you could use to take your front-end that much closer to perfection. Completely free to use, with no strings attached, we desire to help uplift those interested in developing their skillsets.
